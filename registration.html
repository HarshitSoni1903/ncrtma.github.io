Submission Guidelines

Authors willing to present their research work can submit the abstract electronically (not more than 300 words) to ncrtma@gmail.com(on or before 26/12/2016).

All submitted papers will be reviewed by advisory committees of the Conference and a proceeding with all accepted papers will be released on the first day of the conference.

The scrutinized and accepted papers will be published in Reputed Journal with nominal cost.
