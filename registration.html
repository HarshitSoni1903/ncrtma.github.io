Submission Guidelines

Authors willing to present their research work can submit the abstract electronically (not more than 300 words) to ncrtma@gmail.com on or before 23/12/2016.

All submitted papers will be reviewed by advisory committee of the Conference and a proceeding with all accepted papers will be released on the first day of the conference.

The scrutinized and accepted papers will be published in Far East Journal of Mathematical Sciences
(ANNEXURE II,SCOPUS INDEXED) with Concessional Cost.

Registration Fee

Faculty Rs.1000/-
Student & Research Scholars Rs.750/-

No TA / DA will be Provided

At least one of the authors of the selected paper should register and present the paper in the conference.
Registration form is to be sent in the prescribed format along with a Demand Draft (D.D) in favour of
“Department of Basic Sciences,Vadapalani Campus,SRM University” payable at Chennai.
